Welcome!


Type I collagen is the single most abundant protein in mammals and forms the structural basis of all connective tissues. By studying collagen at sub-nanometer resolution in situ, and collagen-extracellular matrix (ECM) interactions in silico, and by making our findings a web-accessible resource, Interact With Collagen will deliver a powerful tool for hypothesis formation and testing.
